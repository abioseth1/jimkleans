Shopping and leisure centers cleaning

JimKlean specialises in cleaning the floors and utilities associated with retail shopping centers. Shopping Centers have a high volume of daily users, from regular traffic to traders which leads to spread of wastes and gems therefore requiring effective daily cleaning, both during opening and after hours.

Typical cleaning services required

When the center is open to the public, attention needs to be paid to providing a clean and safe environment.

  • Bin: waste bins can fill quickly
  • Toilet: thousands of people can use the toilets in a short space of time.
  • Spills: spills can occur frequently making the environment a potential hazard.
  • Wash room: your leisure center’s wash room might experience peak usage

We can supply and restock all wash room facilities (with paper products, soap, air-sanitisers, and sanitary bins), clean your bins and enclosures, and clean your outdoor areas and car parks.

Once the shopping center closes the real cleaning process takes place with specialist equipment such as auto scrubbers, duplex machines, polishers, and steam cleaning equipment required to maintain the centre to a high standard.

Recruitment & Training Process

The Jimklean recruitment process is designed to cover all the requirements of pre-vetting, experience capture, automatic referencing and allows Jimklean to access all required accreditation information.

CV SCREENING

1st Step

At this stage, our expert recruiters look for the right attitude, passion and desire to work.

PHONE INTERVIEW

2nd Step

If the conversation goes well, our consultant books a personal interview for the following days.

FACE TO FACE INTERVIEW

3rd Step

Knowledge about the job, customer service, attitude and experience will be tested during the personal interview.

CV SCREENING

Our consultants analyse every application and CV before accepting or rejecting the applicant.

We take this part very seriously; therefore, although experience is important, transferable skills and potential to be a good member of our team are essential and they can compensate for the lack of experience.

All candidates are contacted whether successful or not.

PHONE INTERVIEW

One of our consultants telephones the applicants to have a conversation with them.The first minutes of the chat are decisive to confirm if the applicant will be a suitable.

Even if they do not have the best experience, their politeness, passion and effort to convince that they are good candidates, will make them likely to pass to the next step of our process. If successful, the applicant will be invited in for a face to face interview.

FACE TO FACE INTERVIEW

The candidate will sit down with one of our recruitment consultants for a formal chat.

Our expert recruiters will try to find their interest in joining our team, as well as measuring their familiarity with the hospitality industry.

We keep a record of it and in case the candidate succeeds, our consultants will know which kind of training will be recommendable for the candidate and which kind of position will be the best and their availability.

If the candidate passes this interview, we will book the first training session available for him or her.
